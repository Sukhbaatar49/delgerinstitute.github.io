About the Institute
The Delger Institute is Mongolia's independent policy research institution, dedicated to strengthening democratic foundations through rigorous, nonpartisan analysis.
Founded 2026
Location Ulaanbaatar
Focus Governance & Democracy
Affiliation Nonpartisan
Mongolia's Most Pressing Policy Challenges
01
Institutional Erosion & Democratic Backsliding
Mongolia's democratic institutions have weakened over time. Identifying the mechanisms of erosion and effective countermeasures is the Institute's core mission.
02
Corruption & the Resource Curse
Mining wealth has fueled both development and corruption. Mongolia must build transparent, accountable governance of its natural resource revenues.
03
Geopolitical Sovereignty Between Giants
Landlocked between Russia and China, Mongolia's ability to preserve genuine independence requires sophisticated, evidence-based foreign policy analysis.

Built to serve Mongolia — not any party, donor, or foreign interest.

The Delger Institute was founded in 2026 by Sukhbaatar Puntsag with a single conviction: that Mongolia's future will be shaped by the quality of its ideas. We produce rigorous, independent research on governance, democracy, and public policy — freely available to every Mongolian citizen.

We are nonpartisan, transparently funded, and committed to intellectual honesty above all else. When our findings are uncomfortable, we publish them anyway.
